# Networkers Home — Interview Questions

> Free database of 100+ networking and cybersecurity interview questions with atomic answers, curated by **Vikas Swami, Dual CCIE #22239**, founder of [Networkers Home](https://www.networkershome.com/).

**🌐 Live: [interviews.networkershome.com](https://interviews.networkershome.com)**

## What's in here

- **100+ real interview questions** from 18 years of Bangalore placement data across 800+ hiring partners
- **4 topic clusters**: Cisco / Network Engineer, AI Cybersecurity, Ethical Hacking, SOC Analyst
- **Atomic answers** (80-120 words each) suitable for quick interview prep
- **Deep-dive links** back to [networkershome.com](https://www.networkershome.com/) for full context, follow-up questions, and strong vs weak answer notes

## Why this site exists

Most networking interview prep is content-farm Q&A written by people who've never actually conducted a technical interview. This database is different — every question comes from real interview rounds at Bangalore's top employers (Cisco, HCL, Akamai, Aryaka, Movate, Wipro, TCS, Infosys, IBM, Accenture + 790 more).
